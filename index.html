Research

I am interested in developing mathematical structure for a variety of topics including uncertainty, combinatorial optimization, stochastic optimization, unified framework for non-linear mathematical physics. I am also interesting in topological application for gradient based optimization for physical systems such as agrivoltaics.

  • Non-linear mathematical physics: I have developed a unified framework for physical systems such as fluid dynamics. Currently, I am translating this to a Geometric framework where the physical systems appear as a effect of geodesic gradient flow.
  • Combinatorial optimization: This project is a bridge between combinatorial optimization and modular forms. The idea is to define a seed function whose lifted form vanishses identically at global optimum. Furthemore, I have developed a three step fourier, Hecke-relations and completed L-function filter to test the candidate tour for global optimality.
  • Stochastic optimization: This is an ongoing ptoject where I use inverse Ricci flow to optimize stochastic problems.
  • Agrivoltaics: This is my PhD work. I also translated the problem of optimizing solar panel tracking algorihtm to finding the optimal random walk on the surface of a torus. It also included chapters on data Analysis, modeling and optimization of Agrivoltaic systems in addition to using a hybrid LSTM-transformer system for predicting plant yields.
